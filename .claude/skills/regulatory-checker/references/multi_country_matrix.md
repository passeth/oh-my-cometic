# Multi-Country Regulatory Comparison Matrix

Comprehensive comparison of cosmetic regulations across major markets: Korea, EU, USA, China, Japan, and ASEAN.

## Regulatory Framework Overview

| Aspect | Korea (MFDS) | EU | USA (FDA) | China (NMPA) | Japan (PMDA) | ASEAN |
|--------|--------------|-----|-----------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| Primary Regulation | Cosmetics Act | EC 1223/2009 | FD&C Act | Cosmetic Supervision Regulations | Pharmaceutical Affairs Law | ASEAN Cosmetic Directive |
| Pre-market Approval | Category-based | Notification | None (except colors) | Registration required | Category-based | Notification |
| Responsible Person | Yes | Yes | N/A | Yes (in China) | Yes | Yes |
| GMP Required | Functional only | ISO 22716 | Voluntary | Yes | Quasi-drug only | Yes |
| Animal Testing | Alternatives accepted | Banned | Not required | Waived for many categories | Limited | Not mandated |

### UV Filter Notes by Region

**Korea:**
- UV filters for functional cosmetics require registration
- SPF/PA testing per MFDS guidelines

**EU:**
- Several UV filters under SCCS re-evaluation
- Nano forms require separate notification
- Spray products may have additional restrictions

**USA:**
- Limited approved UV filters (16 total)
- Many modern UV filters not approved
- Sunscreens regulated as OTC drugs

**China:**
- UV filter list aligned with EU
- Testing required for SPF/PA claims
- Separate sunscreen category registration

**Japan:**
- Most permissive UV filter list
- Quasi-drug classification for high SPF
- PA system for UVA protection

### Preservative Special Restrictions

**EU Recent Changes:**
- MIT banned in leave-on products (2017)
- MCI/MI banned in leave-on products
- Formaldehyde releasers under review

- MIT completely banned (2020)
- Stricter than EU on some formaldehyde releasers

**Japan:**
- Positive list system - only approved preservatives allowed
- Formaldehyde releasers significantly restricted

### Prohibited Claim Types (All Regions)

- Claims to treat, cure, or prevent disease
- Medical or therapeutic claims
- Claims comparing to pharmaceuticals
- Unsubstantiated efficacy claims
- Misleading or false claims

### Claim Substantiation Requirements

| Region | Requirement | Evidence Type |
|--------|-------------|---------------|

| China | Special cosmetics | Efficacy testing required |
| Japan | Quasi-drugs | Clinical evidence |
| ASEAN | Efficacy claims | Supporting evidence |

## Quick Compliance Checklist by Country

### Korea Export Checklist
- [ ] Product category determination (general vs. functional)
- [ ] Ingredient compliance check
- [ ] Korean labeling with required information
- [ ] MFDS notification or registration
- [ ] Korean responsible person designation
- [ ] Safety data compilation

### EU Export Checklist
- [ ] CPNP notification
- [ ] EU Responsible Person designation
- [ ] Product Information File (PIF)
- [ ] Safety assessment by qualified person
- [ ] GMP certification (ISO 22716)
- [ ] Nanomaterial notification (if applicable)
- [ ] EU-compliant labeling

### USA Export Checklist
- [ ] Product classification (cosmetic vs. OTC drug)
- [ ] Ingredient compliance check
- [ ] US labeling requirements
- [ ] Registration (voluntary but recommended)
- [ ] Adverse event reporting system
- [ ] For OTC: Drug monograph compliance

### China Export Checklist
- [ ] Product category (general vs. special)
- [ ] NMPA registration or filing
- [ ] Chinese responsible agent
- [ ] Chinese language labeling
- [ ] Testing at Chinese-approved labs (if required)
- [ ] Animal testing alternatives documentation

### Japan Export Checklist
- [ ] Product classification (cosmetic vs. quasi-drug)
- [ ] Ingredient compliance (positive list)
- [ ] Japanese responsible person
- [ ] Japanese labeling
- [ ] PMDA notification or approval
- [ ] Import notification

### ASEAN Export Checklist
- [ ] Country-specific notification (varies by country)
- [ ] ASEAN Cosmetic Directive compliance
- [ ] Local responsible person per country
- [ ] Language requirements per country
- [ ] Product Information File
- [ ] GMP documentation
