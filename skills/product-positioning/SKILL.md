# Product Positioning Skill

A comprehensive skill for developing and analyzing product positioning strategies in the cosmetic industry.

## Overview

Product positioning is the strategic process of establishing a distinct place in the market for your cosmetic product. This skill provides frameworks, methodologies, and tools to:

- Analyze competitive landscapes
- Develop unique selling propositions (USP)
- Create price-value positioning matrices
- Align products with target audiences
- Establish category positioning (mass to luxury)

## When to Use This Skill

Invoke this skill when:
- Launching a new cosmetic product
- Repositioning an existing product
- Analyzing competitive threats
- Developing pricing strategies
- Creating differentiation strategies
- Building brand architecture

## Core Components

### 1. Positioning Framework Analysis
Systematic approach to defining your product's market position using proven frameworks:
- Perceptual mapping
- Value proposition canvas
- Positioning statement development
- Brand ladder methodology

### 2. Competitive Analysis
Comprehensive competitive landscape mapping:
- Direct competitor identification
- Indirect competitor analysis
- Competitive advantage assessment
- White space opportunity identification

### 3. Price-Value Matrix
Strategic pricing alignment:
- Category tier positioning (mass, masstige, prestige, luxury)
- Price elasticity considerations
- Value perception optimization
- Premium justification strategies

### 4. Target Audience Alignment
Consumer-centric positioning:
- Demographic profiling
- Psychographic analysis
- Behavioral segmentation
- Need-state mapping

## Examples

### Example 1: New Serum Launch Positioning

**Input:**
```
Position a new vitamin C serum at 35,000 KRW targeting women 25-35
Key competitors: Klairs, Some By Mi, By Wishtrend
```

**Output:**
```
Positioning Recommendation:

Category Position: Masstige (accessible premium)
Primary Differentiator: Stabilized vitamin C with proven 28-day visible results
Target Segment: Skinimalist millennials seeking effective, no-fuss skincare

Positioning Statement:
For skincare-conscious millennials who want visible brightening results without complicated routines, [Product] is a stabilized vitamin C serum that delivers clinically-proven brightening in 28 days because of our patented encapsulation technology. Unlike competitors that require refrigeration or have short shelf life, our formula stays potent for 12 months at room temperature.

Competitive Position: Premium efficacy at accessible price point
White Space: Long-lasting stability + proven results + minimalist positioning
```

## Best Practices

1. **Always validate positioning with data** - Use WebSearch to verify market assumptions
2. **Consider multiple dimensions** - Position on both functional and emotional benefits
3. **Test positioning internally first** - Ensure alignment across organization
4. **Monitor and adjust** - Positioning should evolve with market dynamics
5. **Localize appropriately** - Adapt positioning for different markets (Korea, China, SEA)
