# Price-Value Positioning Matrix for Cosmetics

## Overview

Price positioning is a critical strategic decision that affects brand perception, competitive dynamics, and profitability. This guide provides frameworks for aligning price with value perception in the cosmetic market.

### Price Index Calculation

```
Price Index = (Our Price / Average Competitor Price) x 100

Example:
Our serum: 50,000 KRW
Competitor average: 55,000 KRW
Price Index: (50,000 / 55,000) x 100 = 91

Interpretation:
< 90: Aggressively priced
90-95: Competitively priced
95-105: Market-aligned
105-115: Premium priced
> 115: Super-premium
```
