## Compliance Check Process

### Step 1: Ingredient Screening
1. List all ingredients with INCI names
2. Check against prohibited lists
3. Identify restricted ingredients

### Step 2: Concentration Verification
1. Compare concentrations to maximum limits
2. Note any exceedances
3. Identify reformulation needs

### Step 3: Documentation
1. Prepare compliance certificates
2. Document ingredient sources
3. Maintain safety data

### Step 4: Labeling Review
1. Check allergen declarations
2. Verify warning statements
3. Confirm country-specific requirements
